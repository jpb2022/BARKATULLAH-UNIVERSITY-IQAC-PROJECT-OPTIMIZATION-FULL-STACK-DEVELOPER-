Welcome to the Self Study Report (SSR)

Barkatullah Vishwavidyalaya has completed its Self Study Report (SSR) for accreditation purposes. This report provides a detailed account of the university’s activities, institutional preparedness, and achievements in various areas such as academic excellence, infrastructure, research, and student welfare.

SSR Final

The final SSR document contains detailed information for the NAAC accreditation process. It includes qualitative and quantitative metrics, along with a self-assessment report of the university's performance over the years.

Executive Summary

This section summarizes the highlights of the SSR, providing an overview of the university’s academic achievements, infrastructure, and social contributions.

Evaluative Reports of the Department

The department-wise evaluative reports assess the quality of education, research output, faculty performance, and student satisfaction in each department.

Data Templates Document

This document contains the various data templates for reporting quantitative metrics that are essential for accreditation purposes.

Quantitative Metric (QnM)

The Quantitative Metric (QnM) focuses on numerical data, performance indicators, and key metrics for evaluating the university's growth and achievements in academic, research, and infrastructure sectors.

IQAC Minutes of the Meeting

Minutes of the IQAC meetings, which detail the discussions, decisions, and action plans made for enhancing the quality of education and research.
