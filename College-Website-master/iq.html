Welcome to the Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Our mission is to develop and implement quality benchmarks in academic and administrative activities, promote student-centric learning approaches, encourage innovation, research, and ensure alignment with the highest standards of accreditation bodies.

Vision

To be a world-class university for social transformation through dynamic education.

Mission

  • To develop and implement quality benchmarks in academic and administrative activities.
  • To promote student-centric learning approaches and ensure holistic development.
  • To encourage innovation, research, and collaboration with stakeholders.
  • To align institutional practices with the highest standards of accreditation bodies.
