About IQAC

The Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at Global Skills University works to maintain and enhance the quality of education provided to students. It helps in developing a system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic improvement in the overall academic, administrative, and research quality of the university.

Mission of IQAC

Our mission is to continuously improve the quality of academic programs, research, infrastructure, and student services through strategic planning and implementing reforms in all areas of the university's functioning.

Objectives

  • Promote quality education and training.
  • Enhance faculty development and innovation.
  • Ensure effective student support services.
  • Improve administrative processes for efficiency.
  • Facilitate collaborations and partnerships with academic institutions.
