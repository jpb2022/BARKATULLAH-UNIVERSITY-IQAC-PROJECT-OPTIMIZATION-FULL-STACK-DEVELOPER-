Internal Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC)

Welcome to the IQAC of IIT Kgp

Vision

To be a world class University for Social Transformation through Dynamic Education

Mission

  • To develop and implement quality benchmarks in academic and administrative activities.
  • To promote student-centric learning approaches and ensure holistic development.
  • To encourage innovation, research, and collaboration with stakeholders.
  • To align institutional practices with the highest standards of accreditation bodies.

Objectives

  • To institutionalize a system for conscious, consistent, and catalytic improvement.
  • To enhance the academic and administrative performance of the college.
  • To foster a learner-centric environment for quality education and skill enhancement.
  • To promote stakeholder engagement and integrate their feedback into the quality assurance process.

Functions of IQAC

  • Preparation and submission of the Annual Quality Assurance Report (AQAR) to NAAC.
  • Establishing quality benchmarks for institutional practices.
  • Conducting regular academic and administrative audits.
  • Organizing seminars, workshops, and faculty development programs (FDPs).
  • Collecting and analyzing feedback from students, faculty, and other stakeholders.
  • Encouraging the use of modern teaching-learning methods and technology.
  • Coordinating activities related to institutional accreditation and rankings.
